The Haskell Foundation (HF)

An independent, non-profit organization dedicated to broadening the adoption of Haskell, by supporting its ecosystem of tools, libraries, education, and research.

We're getting started!

Watch Simon Peyton Jones announce the Haskell Foundation (HF) at the Haskell eXchange conference on November 4, 2020.

In this talk, Simon talks about why we are launching this new organization and its goals. At the end, he is joined by a panel of HF volunteers to take questions from the audience.

not “just another programming language”

Haskell is not “just another programming language”: it embodies a radical and elegant attack on the entire enterprise of writing software. It profoundly influences the world of software for the better.
